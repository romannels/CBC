Where the Bible is believed and taught!
 Welcome to the website of Cornerstone Bible Church in Crete, Nebraska!

      As you visit our site, we hope you find what you are looking for. We believe a relationship with the Living God through Jesus Christ provides the ultimate and only meaningful solution for mankind's many needs. And we know that solution can only be found in God's Word, which is why we preach the Word here. 

Thank you for visiting our website today. For your next step, we would like to invite you to visit us in person, and we look forward to seeing you. Until then, we look forward to be found in Him, hearing "well done good and faithful servant."
